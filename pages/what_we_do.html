Performance Pricing Models

CPI

Cost Per Install – Pay only when your app is installed by users.

  • Fraud-protected traffic
  • Real-user installs
  • OEM & SDK inventory

CPS

Cost Per Sale – You’re billed only when a verified sale occurs.

  • Ideal for e-commerce
  • Performance-based billing
  • Detailed sale tracking

CPA

Cost Per Action – Pay when users take your desired action.

  • Sign-ups or form fills
  • Custom actions supported
  • Full conversion visibility

CPM

Cost Per Mille – Pay a flat rate for every 1,000 ad impressions.

  • Great for brand awareness
  • High volume reach
  • Impression-based exposure
